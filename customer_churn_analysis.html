01 Key Contributions
🗄️ Analyzed a dataset of 5,000+ customer records to identify key factors influencing customer churn and retention across multiple demographics and service tiers.
🧹 Performed data cleaning and preprocessing, reducing missing and inconsistent data by 20% using Python (Pandas) and SQL validation queries.
📊 Conducted Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) using Pandas, NumPy, and Matplotlib to uncover customer behavior trends including tenure, contract type, and service usage patterns.
📈 Created interactive dashboards in Power BI to visualize KPIs, churn rates, and customer demographics with drill-through filters for dynamic business exploration.
💡 Generated actionable insights highlighting factors associated with a 15% higher churn probability, supporting data-driven decisions for customer retention strategy.

03 Actionable Insights
📋 Customers on month-to-month contracts showed the highest churn rate (72%), indicating a need for long-term contract incentives.
🕐 New customers with tenure under 6 months were 15% more likely to churn — suggesting a critical onboarding window.
💳 Customers using electronic check payments had the highest churn probability versus auto-payment users.
🛡️ Customers without tech support or online security add-ons were significantly more likely to leave.
